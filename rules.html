ReefMC Network Rules

Welcome to the official ReefMC Network Rules. The server is intended to be a fun, safe, and friendly environment for all players to enjoy. To maintain a positive and safe environment for everyone, we have created a set of rules for all players to follow. We may change these rules without notice — it is your responsibility as a player to keep up with them. Keep in mind that we may also punish anyone at our discretion if we believe it to be in the best interest of the server.

Account Safety

  • All players are responsible for their own accounts and personal information. Players are responsible for everything that may occur on their account.
  • We will not lift punishments due to accounts being compromised or accounts being used by other people to break rules.
  • Do not share your account with anyone else!

Allowed Modifications

  1. Due to the large amount of mods available, we aren't able to give a comprehensive list of allowed or disallowed modifications. A general rule of thumb is that any modification that provides a significant advantage to the player is against our rules. Additionally, any mods that automate player gameplay are strictly prohibited, be those Minecraft modifications, external software, or hardware.
Warning: Make sure you always install mods from official sources such as Modrinth, CurseForge, the mod creator's website, GitHub, or other verified profiles (e.g. YouTube). Unofficial downloads may not work properly or may contain malicious code.

Disallowed Modifications

  • Anything (including external applications) that changes keyboard repeat rate
    • For example: Filter Keys
  • Any mods that give an unfair advantage in PVP, such as:
    • Reach
    • Fly
    • Killaura
    • Aimbot
    • etc.
  • Automation mods such as Baritone, macros, and autoclickers
  • X-ray mods and texture packs
  • Litematica Printer
  • Freecam
  • No Mining Cooldown
  • View Lock
  • ClickCrystals
  • Older Forge versions (1.9.* → 1.19.*)
  • Inventory mods, such as:
    • Elytra Swapper
    • Autototem (and Autodoublehand and LegitAutototem)
    • Autoequip (automatically equips armor in your inventory)
    • Autoswitch (automatically mines with the correct tool)
    • Inventory Walk/Move
    • Mouse Wheelie and Item Scroller
    • Ghost Item Giver
  • All crystal optimizers apart from Marlow's Crystal Optimizer and CDAGaming's Crystal Optimizer
  • All anchor optimizers apart from Herobane's Anchor Optimizer
  • Minimaps that show entities
  • VR mods
  • Mods that show invisible players' nametags
  • Mods that show invisible players' health (including mob healthbars)
  • Librarian Trade Finder

Allowed Modifications

  • Mousetweaks
  • Optimization mods such as Optifine, Sodium, etc.
  • Clients such as Feather and Lunar Client
  • Free Look
  • HUD mods (like Armor Status, Effect Status, etc.)
  • Minimaps that don't show players
  • Cosmetic mods (capes, time changer, brightness, etc.)
  • Gameplay recorder mods (ReplayMod, etc.) — not allowed on Lifesteal or Money Dupe!
  • Mods that allow the use of controllers
    • Note: mods that include AimAssist are NOT allowed.

📚 Chat Abuse

  • Do not spam chat with repeated/similar messages in a short period of time.
  • Do not flood chat with extremely long messages, non-English characters, or gibberish.
  • Do not attempt to bypass the chat filter. If you wish to see profanity, you may toggle the chat filter for yourself in /settings.
  • Extreme toxicity or being rude to other players is not allowed.
  • Do not riot for/against a particular issue, decision, or topic.

🎮 Unfair Advantages

  • Any disallowed game modifications (such as cheats or blacklisted mods) or external programs (such as macros, scripts) are not allowed on the network.
  • AFKing farms using auto-clickers, macros, scripts, or similar methods is not allowed.
  • Abusing bugs to gain an advantage over other players is not allowed.
  • Texture packs that allow you to see through blocks are not allowed.
  • We may conduct screenshares to make sure players are not using any blacklisted modifications. Refusing to screenshare will result in a punishment.

⚖️ Discrimination

  • Discriminating against others based on age, disability, gender, race, religion, belief, sex, or sexual orientation is not allowed.
  • Any type of sexism, racism, homophobia, or other discrimination will not be tolerated.

⚠️ Threats

  • Threatening to, or actively participating in, DDoSing, doxing, blackmailing, swatting, distributing malware or malicious links, or IP-grabbing is strictly prohibited.
  • Any jokes about the aforementioned topics are also prohibited.
  • Wishing death or harm on other players or their family members is strictly prohibited.

❌ Inappropriate Content

  • Discussing highly offensive topics, such as anything sexual, drug-related, political, or hateful, is not considered appropriate for the server.
  • Inappropriate or offensive usernames, skins, capes, item names, structures/builds, or any other cosmetics are not allowed.

📢 Advertisement

  • Do not self-promote, or promote other servers or Discord servers, on the network.
  • Advertising videos or streams is fine, as long as the content is ReefMC related.

🚀 Boosting

  • Using another account to intentionally boost stats such as kills is not allowed.

🚫 Punishment Evasion

  • Do not evade punishments by using alternative accounts or other methods such as renaming items.
  • Muted players are allowed to ask other players to speak on their behalf.
